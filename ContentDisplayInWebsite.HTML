Pizza

Pizza is one of the most loved foods in the world! 🍕 Here are some fun facts about it: Fun Facts About Pizza 🍕 Ancient origins – The first version of pizza dates back to ancient Greece, where people topped flatbreads with oil, herbs, and cheese. 🍕 Margherita pizza was made for a queen – In 1889, a chef in Naples created the Margherita pizza (tomato, mozzarella, basil) for Queen Margherita of Italy. The colors represent the Italian flag! 🇮🇹 🍕 World’s biggest pizza? – The largest pizza ever made covered 13,580 square feet (1,261 square meters)! 🍕😲 🍕 Most expensive pizza – A luxury pizza in Italy costs $12,000 because it’s topped with lobster, caviar, and edible gold! 🍕 People love pizza A LOT – Over 5 billion pizzas are sold every year worldwide! 🍕 Most popular topping? – Pepperoni is the most loved pizza topping in the U.S. and many other places. 🍕 Hawaiian pizza was invented in Canada! – Despite its name, Hawaiian pizza (pineapple & ham) was created by a Canadian chef in 1962. 🍕 Pizza Hut delivered to space! – In 2001, Pizza Hut sent a pizza to astronauts on the International Space Station. 🚀
